<p><p>The tea finishing machine market includes various technologies that enhance tea processing. Roller finishing machines mechanically manipulate leaves, improving shape and flavor. Microwave finishing machines utilize electromagnetic waves to rapidly heat and process tea, preserving freshness. Steam finishing machines use steam to halt oxidation, ensuring a vibrant color and robust taste. Lastly, hot wind finishing machines circulate hot air to dry and refine tea leaves efficiently, promoting a consistent product. Each type caters to different production needs and quality standards.</p></p>
