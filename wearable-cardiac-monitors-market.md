<p><strong>The Wearable Cardiac Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Pulse Based</li><li>Optical Technology Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wearable cardiac monitors are advancing in two main types: electric pulse-based and optical technology-based. Electric pulse-based monitors operate by detecting electrical signals from the heart, providing precise data on cardiac activity, such as arrhythmias. In contrast, optical technology-based monitors utilize light sensors to measure blood flow and heart rate through the skin. This non-invasive approach offers convenience and ease of use. Both types cater to different patient needs, contributing to the growing demand for continuous heart health monitoring.</p></p>
