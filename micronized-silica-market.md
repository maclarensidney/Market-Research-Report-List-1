<p><ul><li>Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Semi Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Undensified Silica Fume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micromized silica market is categorized into three main types: Densified Silica Fume, Semi-Densified Silica Fume, and Undensified Silica Fume. Densified Silica Fume features a compact structure, offering high strength and minimal dust, ideal for concrete and construction applications. Semi-Densified Silica Fume presents a balance between density and flowability, suitable for various industrial uses. Undensified Silica Fume has a lightweight and fluffy texture, often employed in applications where high surface area is required for better reactivity and performance.</p></p>
