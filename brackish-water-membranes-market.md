<p><p>Brackish water membranes are specialized filtration systems designed to treat brackish water, which is a mix of fresh and saltwater with lower salinity than seawater. These membranes use reverse osmosis (RO) technology to remove dissolved salts, impurities, and contaminants, making the water suitable for industrial, agricultural, and potable use. They are widely used in desalination plants, wastewater treatment, and water purification systems.</p><p>The Brackish Water Membranes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period. <p><p>The **Brackish Water Membranes Market** includes key types like **Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane**, which removes dissolved salts and impurities using high pressure; **Nanofiltration (NF) Membrane**, which selectively filters divalent ions and organic molecules; **Forward Osmosis (FO) Membrane**, which uses osmotic pressure for low-energy separation; and **Electrodialysis (ED) Membrane**, which separates ions via electric fields. Each type serves specific applications in desalination, wastewater treatment, and industrial processes, addressing varying salinity levels and operational requirements in brackish water treatment.</p></p>
