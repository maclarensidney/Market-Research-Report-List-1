<p><strong>The Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Drainage Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventricular Drainage System</li><li>Lumbar Drainage System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) drainage systems are essential for managing conditions like hydrocephalus and increased intracranial pressure. The ventricular drainage system involves catheter placement within the brain's ventricles to drain excess CSF, effectively reducing pressure and preventing damage. In contrast, the lumbar drainage system utilizes a catheter inserted in the lower spine, allowing for CSF removal or sampling. Both systems cater to specific clinical needs, with applications in neurosurgery, critical care, and neurology, driving their market growth and innovation.</p></p>
