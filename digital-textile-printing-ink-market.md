<p><p>The digital textile printing ink market comprises various types tailored for different fabrics and applications. Reactive dye inks bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and cellulosic materials. Acidic inks are suited for protein fibers like silk and wool, providing vibrant colors. Paint inks offer versatility for diverse substrates and surfaces, while dispersion and sublimation inks excel in printing on synthetic fabrics, transferring vibrant designs through heat application. Each ink type caters to specific needs, enhancing color quality and durability in textile printing.</p></p>
