<p><ul><li>One Piece Pouch</li><li>Two Piece Pouch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urostomy pouch market features two main types: one-piece and two-piece pouches. A one-piece pouch integrates the adhesive skin barrier and collection bag into a single unit, simplifying application and maintenance, making it user-friendly for patients. In contrast, a two-piece pouch consists of a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for more flexibility and easier replacement of the bag without removing the barrier. Both types serve to collect urine after urostomy surgery, catering to different patient preferences and needs.</p></p>
