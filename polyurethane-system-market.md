<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Type</li><li>Thermosetting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane system market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) can be remolded and reshaped when heated, offering versatility for applications like flexible seals and coatings. In contrast, thermosetting polyurethane forms permanent bonds during curing, making it ideal for rigid products such as foams, adhesives, and coatings that require durability and chemical resistance. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and furniture, driving innovation and growth in the market.</p></p>
