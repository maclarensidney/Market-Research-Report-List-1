<p><strong>The Liquid Nitrogen Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volume Flow Meter</li><li>Mass Flowmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid nitrogen flow meter market is categorized mainly into volume flow meters and mass flow meters. Volume flow meters measure the quantity of liquid nitrogen passing through a system over time, typically using mechanisms like turbine or magnetic principles. In contrast, mass flow meters gauge the mass of liquid nitrogen, considering its density and temperature for accurate readings, often employing Coriolis or thermal methods. Both types are essential in various applications, including industrial, aerospace, and medical settings.</p></p>
