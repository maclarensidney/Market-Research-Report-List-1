<p><p>High-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) therapy is classified into two main market types: non-invasive and minimally invasive treatments. Non-invasive HIFU involves external application of ultrasound waves, targeting tissues without incisions, making it suitable for procedures like facial rejuvenation and cancer treatment. Conversely, minimally invasive HIFU typically involves small incisions or endoscopic techniques to deliver targeted treatment, enhancing precision while reducing recovery time. Both types leverage ultrasound energy to effectively deliver therapeutic effects, offering diverse options for patients.</p></p>
