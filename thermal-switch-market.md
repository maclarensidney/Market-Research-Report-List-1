<p><strong>The Thermal Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normally Open Thermal Switch</li><li>Normally Closed Thermal Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal switch market features two primary types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC) thermal switches. Normally Open thermal switches remain disconnected until a predefined temperature is reached, activating the circuit to allow current flow. Conversely, Normally Closed thermal switches are connected by default and break the circuit when the temperature exceeds a set limit, halting current flow. These switches are widely used in various applications, including HVAC systems and appliances, ensuring efficient thermal management and safety.</p></p>
