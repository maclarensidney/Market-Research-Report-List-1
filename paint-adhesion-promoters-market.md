<p><p>The Paint Adhesion Promoters market is divided into two main types: waterborne and oil-based adhesion promoters. Waterborne paint adhesion promoters are environmentally friendly, providing excellent adhesion in applications requiring low VOCs. They are widely used in automotive and industrial coatings. Conversely, oil-based paint adhesion promoters offer strong bonding properties for heavier substrates and are often used in construction and wood finishing. Each type serves specific needs, influencing the overall performance and longevity of coatings in various applications.</p></p>
