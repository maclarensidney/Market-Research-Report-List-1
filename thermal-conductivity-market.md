<p><p>The thermal conductivity market is segmented into three primary methods: Heat Wire, Light Flash, and Heat Flow. </p><p>1. **Heat Wire Method** involves inserting a heating wire into a material and measuring temperature changes to determine thermal conductivity.</p><p>2. **Light Flash Method** uses a short pulse of light to heat the material's surface, analyzing the resulting temperature rise.</p><p>3. **Heat Flow Method**, also known as the guarded hot plate method, measures the heat transfer through a material under controlled conditions. Each method serves diverse applications across industries, catering to specific material properties.</p></p>
