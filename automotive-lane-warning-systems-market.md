As automotive manufacturers focus on enhancing safety and reducing accidents, the demand for lane warning systems is expected to continue rising globally.</p></p>

<p><p>Automotive Lane Warning Systems enhance road safety by alerting drivers to unintended lane departures. **Lane Departure Warning (LDW)** uses cameras to detect lane markings and issues alerts if the vehicle drifts without signaling. **Lane Keeping System (LKS)** goes further by actively steering the vehicle back into the lane if the driver doesn’t respond to warnings. **Other systems** may include advanced features like lane centering or adaptive assistance, combining sensors and AI for smoother, safer driving experiences. These technologies aim to reduce accidents caused by lane deviations.</p></p>
