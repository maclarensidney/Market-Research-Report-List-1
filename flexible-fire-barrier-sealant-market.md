<p><strong>The Flexible Fire Barrier Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Fire Barrier Sealant market comprises primarily two types: elastomeric and intumescent. Elastomeric sealants are flexible, durable, and provide excellent adhesion, making them suitable for dynamic joint applications, effectively sealing gaps and resisting smoke and fire. Intumescent sealants, on the other hand, expand when exposed to heat, forming a char that blocks fire and smoke, providing enhanced passive fire protection. Both types are crucial for maintaining safety in buildings by ensuring fire compartmentalization and compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
