<p><p>The multi-mode microplate readers market includes three key types. Filter-based readers utilize specific optical filters to select wavelengths for measuring light absorbance, fluorescence, or luminescence. Monochromator-based readers offer more flexibility by using adjustable monochromators to select a wide range of wavelengths, enabling detailed analysis. Hybrid readers combine both filter and monochromator technologies, allowing for versatile applications and enhanced performance. This diversity addresses varied research needs in laboratories, pharmaceuticals, and diagnostics, driving the growth of the microplate readers market.</p></p>
