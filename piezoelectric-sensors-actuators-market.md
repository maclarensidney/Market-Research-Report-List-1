<p><p>The piezoelectric sensors and actuators market is categorized into two main types: piezoelectric sensors and piezoelectric actuators. Piezoelectric sensors detect changes in pressure, acceleration, or force by converting mechanical energy into electrical signals, making them vital for applications in healthcare, automotive, and industrial sectors. Conversely, piezoelectric actuators utilize electrical energy to generate mechanical movement, enabling precise control in robotics, micro-manipulation, and sound devices. Together, they play crucial roles in advancing technology across various industries.</p></p>
