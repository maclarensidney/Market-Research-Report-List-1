<p><ul><li>Low Permeability</li><li>High Permeability</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF microwave absorber foam is categorized into two main types based on permeability: low permeability and high permeability. Low permeability foams are designed to block electromagnetic waves effectively, making them suitable for applications requiring strong signal absorption, such as in anechoic chambers or radar systems. Conversely, high permeability foams are designed to accommodate the penetration of electromagnetic waves, providing greater flexibility in applications where moderate absorption is needed, such as in mobile communication devices and consumer electronics.</p></p>
