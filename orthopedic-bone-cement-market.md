<p><p>The orthopedic bone cement market is categorized into four main types based on viscosity and functionality. Medium viscosity bone cements offer a balance of handling characteristics and strength, suitable for various applications. High viscosity bone cements provide enhanced stability and are ideal for load-bearing situations. Antibiotic bone cements are infused with antimicrobial agents to prevent infection, making them particularly beneficial in orthopedic surgeries.</p></p>
