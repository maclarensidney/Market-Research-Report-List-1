<p><strong>The Laser Warning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Laser Warning System</li><li>Active Laser Warning System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Warning System (LWS) market includes **Passive** and **Active** systems. **Passive Laser Warning Systems** detect laser threats by sensing emitted radiation without emitting signals, making them stealthy and energy-efficient. They are ideal for covert operations. **Active Laser Warning Systems** actively emit signals to detect and analyze laser threats, offering higher accuracy and faster response times. They are suited for environments requiring real-time threat identification. Both systems enhance situational awareness and protection against laser-guided threats in military and defense applications.</p></p>
