<p><strong>The Aircraft Anti-icing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic De-icing Boots</li><li>Electro-thermal</li><li>Electro-mechanical</li><li>TKS Ice Protection</li><li>Bleed Air</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft anti-icing system market encompasses several types aimed at preventing ice accumulation. Pneumatic de-icing boots use inflatable rubber membranes to break ice. Electro-thermal systems apply heat through electrical elements to melt ice. Electro-mechanical systems utilize mechanical means to remove ice. TKS ice protection employs a de-icing fluid distributed over the wings. Bleed air systems harness compressed air from engines to prevent freezing. Passive systems rely on aerodynamic design to minimize ice formation naturally, enhancing safety and performance.</p></p>
