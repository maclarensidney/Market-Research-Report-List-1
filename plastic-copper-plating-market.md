<p><ul><li>Plating</li><li>Electroless Plating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic copper plating market is divided into two main types: electroplating and electroless plating. Electroplating involves immersing plastic substrates in a solution containing metal ions, with an electrical current applied to deposit copper onto the surface. In contrast, electroless plating relies on a chemical reduction process that deposits copper uniformly without electricity, ensuring even coverage on complex shapes. Both methods are essential for enhancing electrical conductivity, improving corrosion resistance, and enabling various applications in electronics and automotive industries.</p></p>
