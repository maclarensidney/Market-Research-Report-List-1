<p><p>The EEG systems market is categorized into three main types: Conventional EEG, Video EEG, and Dynamic EEG. Conventional EEG focuses on standard brainwave monitoring for diagnosing conditions like epilepsy. Video EEG combines real-time video with EEG data, enhancing the analysis of seizure activity and aiding in accurate diagnosis. Dynamic EEG offers continuous monitoring capabilities, often in home settings, providing insights into brain function over extended periods. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, contributing to advancements in neurology and patient care.</p></p>
