<p><ul><li>Heavy Calcium Carbonate Powder</li><li>Light Calcium Carbonate Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The limestone powder market primarily consists of two types: Heavy Calcium Carbonate (HCC) Powder and Light Calcium Carbonate (LCC) Powder. HCC is produced by mechanically grinding natural limestone, offering high purity and stability, widely used in construction, paints, and plastics. LCC is chemically synthesized, featuring finer particles and higher brightness, making it ideal for paper, rubber, and pharmaceuticals. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, with HCC excelling in bulk applications and LCC in specialized, high-quality requirements.</p></p>
