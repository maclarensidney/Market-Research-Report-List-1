<p><p>The Auto Grade LiDAR Scanner market is primarily divided into two types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR utilizes rotating components to capture detailed 3D maps, offering high-resolution data but with increased complexity and potential wear over time. In contrast, Solid State LiDAR is more compact and robust, relying on solid-state components for sensing, which enhances reliability and reduces maintenance. Both technologies are pivotal in advancing autonomous vehicles and enhancing environmental scanning capabilities.</p></p>
