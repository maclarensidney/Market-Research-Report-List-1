<p><p>The Smart Depalletizing market comprises two main types: Vision Software and Unstacking Robots. Vision Software utilizes advanced imaging techniques to identify and locate products on pallets, guiding robots in the depalletization process with precision. This technology enhances accuracy and efficiency in sorting items. Unstacking Robots, on the other hand, are automated systems designed to physically remove items from pallets, streamlining operations in warehouses and manufacturing environments. Together, these solutions optimize logistics, reduce labor costs, and improve productivity in supply chains.</p></p>
