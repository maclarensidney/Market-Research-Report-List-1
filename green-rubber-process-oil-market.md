<p><p>The Green Rubber Process Oil market is distinguished by two main types based on Polycyclic Aromatic Hydrocarbon (PAH) content: high content and low content. High content PAH process oils contain elevated levels of aromatic compounds, offering enhanced performance in rubber processing, but may raise environmental and health concerns. Conversely, low content PAH process oils are formulated to minimize harmful PAHs, aligning with stringent regulatory standards. Both types cater to different customer preferences and industries focused on sustainable practices and cleaner production methods.</p></p>
