<p><p>The passenger car brake caliper market is primarily divided into two types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize a single piston to apply force on the brake pads, offering a simpler design and cost-effectiveness, typically found in compact vehicles. Multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons for more uniform pressure distribution, enhancing braking performance and heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance and luxury vehicles. Both types cater to different vehicle specifications and performance needs.</p></p>
