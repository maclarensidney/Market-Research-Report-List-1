<p><p>The automatic stretch wrapper market comprises three main types: Rotary Stretch Wrappers, Turntable Stretch Wrappers, and Robotic Stretch Wrappers. Rotary Stretch Wrappers utilize a rotating arm to wrap products, providing high efficiency for large volumes. Turntable Stretch Wrappers feature a rotating platform that securely spins products for wrapping, ideal for varying load sizes. Robotic Stretch Wrappers employ robotic arms for flexible, automated wrapping, suitable for diverse packaging needs and environments. Each type caters to specific operational requirements and packaging processes.</p></p>
