C Type devices operate by utilizing a continuous wave laser and achieve Q-switching through acoustic signals, enabling precise control over pulse characteristics. In contrast, B Type devices are designed for pulsed lasers and provide faster switching speeds, facilitating higher repetition rates. Both types are essential in various applications, including solid-state lasers and industrial laser systems, enhancing performance and efficiency across diverse sectors.</p></p>
