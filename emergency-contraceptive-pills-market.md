<p><strong>The Emergency Contraceptive Pills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Levonorgestrel</li><li>Ulipristal Acetate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency contraceptive pills (ECPs) market primarily comprises two types: Levonorgestrel and Ulipristal Acetate. Levonorgestrel, a progestin, is commonly used in single or dual-dose regimens to prevent ovulation and is effective within 72 hours after unprotected intercourse. Ulipristal Acetate, a selective progesterone receptor modulator, offers a longer window of effectiveness, up to 120 hours post-intercourse, and is particularly effective during the luteal phase of the menstrual cycle. Both types are crucial for reproductive health and family planning.</p></p>
