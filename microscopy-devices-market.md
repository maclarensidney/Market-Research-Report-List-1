<p><p>The microscopy devices market encompasses various types, each serving unique purposes. Scanning Probe Microscopes provide high-resolution surface imaging at the nanoscale by scanning a probe over the specimen. Electron Microscopes utilize electron beams to achieve magnifications far surpassing optical systems, making them ideal for detailed internal structure examination. Optical Microscopes employ visible light to magnify specimens, commonly used in labs and education. Other microscopy methods include fluorescence, confocal, and ion beam techniques, enhancing capabilities across diverse applications.</p></p>
