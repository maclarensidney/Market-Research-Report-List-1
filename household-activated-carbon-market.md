<p><ul><li>Coal-Based Activated Carbon</li><li>Wood-Based Activated Carbon</li><li>Coconut-Based Activated Carbon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household activated carbon market features three primary types: coal-based, wood-based, and coconut-based activated carbon. Coal-based activated carbon is known for its high adsorption capacity and is often used in air and water purification applications. Wood-based activated carbon is lighter and ideal for applications requiring a faster adsorption rate, such as in filters. Coconut-based activated carbon is prized for its sustainable sourcing and fine pore structure, making it effective for both air and water filtration, particularly in residential settings.</p></p>
