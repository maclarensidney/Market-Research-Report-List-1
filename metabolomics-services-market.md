<p><p>Metabolomics services are categorized into targeted and untargeted metabolomics. Targeted metabolomics focuses on quantifying specific metabolites in a sample, providing detailed insights into known metabolic pathways and facilitating precise biomarker discovery. In contrast, untargeted metabolomics aims to profile all metabolites within a biological sample without prior knowledge of their identity, enabling a comprehensive understanding of metabolic changes. Both approaches serve vital roles in research, drug development, and personalized medicine, driving advancements in diagnostics and therapeutic strategies.</p></p>
