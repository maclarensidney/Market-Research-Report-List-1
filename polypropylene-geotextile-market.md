<p><strong>The Polypropylene Geotextile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Woven</li><li>Woven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene geotextile market primarily consists of two types: non-woven and woven geotextiles. Non-woven geotextiles are made from bonded fibers, offering excellent drainage, filtration, and erosion control, making them ideal for applications like landfills and road construction. Woven geotextiles, constructed from interlaced strips of polypropylene, provide superior strength and stability, suitable for applications requiring reinforcement and separation, such as retaining walls and slope stabilization. Together, these types serve diverse engineering and environmental applications.</p></p>
