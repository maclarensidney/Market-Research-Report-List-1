<p><p>The pneumococcal vaccination market includes three main types: PPSV23, PCV7/13, and PCV10. PPSV23 is a polysaccharide vaccine protecting against 23 pneumococcal strains, primarily for adults and high-risk populations. PCV7 and PCV13 are conjugate vaccines targeting 7 and 13 strains, respectively, effective in infants and young children, offering broader protection and inducing a stronger immune response. PCV10 covers 10 strains and is similar to PCV13 but excludes specific serotypes, focusing on areas with specific disease prevalence.</p></p>
