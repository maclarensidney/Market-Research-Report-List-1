<p><strong>The Recombinant Antibody Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chimeric Antibody</li><li>Humanized Antibody</li><li>Full Human Antibody</li><li>Single Chain Antibody</li><li>Bispecific Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recombinant antibody market comprises various types, including chimeric antibodies, which combine mouse and human components to improve immune response; humanized antibodies, which are mostly human but retain some mouse elements to enhance efficacy; and full human antibodies, derived entirely from human sources, minimizing immune reactions. Single-chain antibodies consist of a single polypeptide chain for easier production and flexibility. Bispecific antibodies can simultaneously bind two different antigens, offering advanced therapeutic potential in targeting diseases.</p></p>
