<p><ul><li>Solventborne UV Resins</li><li>100% Solids UV Resins</li><li>Waterborne UV Resins</li><li>Powder UV Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV resin market consists of various types, each catering to specific applications. Solventborne UV resins use solvents to enhance application properties but can emit volatile organic compounds. 100% solids UV resins contain no solvents, offering high-performance coatings and adhesives with minimal environmental impact. Waterborne UV resins mix water with resins for easier application and lower toxicity. Powder UV resins are fine particles that are cured with UV light, providing durable coatings ideal for diverse industrial uses.</p></p>
