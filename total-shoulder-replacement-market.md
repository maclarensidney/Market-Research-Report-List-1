<p><strong>The Total Shoulder Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatomic Total Shoulder Replacements</li><li>Reverse Total Shoulder Replacements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The total shoulder replacement market is primarily divided into two types: Anatomic Total Shoulder Replacements (ATSR) and Reverse Total Shoulder Replacements (RTSR). ATSR mimics the natural anatomy of the shoulder, preserving the rotator cuff’s function, making it suitable for patients with intact rotator cuffs. In contrast, RTSR reverses the ball-and-socket structure to alleviate pain and improve mobility in patients with severe rotator cuff damage or arthritis, enhancing stability and allowing for greater range of motion.</p></p>
