<p><p>A metal plain bearing, also known as a bushing, is a type of bearing that operates on sliding motion between two surfaces without the use of rolling elements. It is typically made from metals such as bronze, steel, or aluminum alloys and is used to reduce friction and wear in machinery. These bearings are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, construction, and industrial machinery due to their durability, load-bearing capacity, and ability to operate under high temperatures and harsh conditions.</p><p>The Metal Plain Bearing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
