<p><p>The healthcare disinfectant and sanitizer market is categorized into two main types: disinfectants and sanitizers. Disinfectants are powerful agents designed to eliminate a broad spectrum of pathogens, including bacteria and viruses, on inanimate surfaces, making them essential for infection control in healthcare settings. Sanitizers, on the other hand, reduce the number of microorganisms to safe levels on surfaces and skin, making them suitable for less contaminated areas. Both play critical roles in maintaining hygiene and preventing the spread of infections in healthcare environments.</p></p>
