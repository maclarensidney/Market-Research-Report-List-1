High Ce polishing powder, with a higher cerium concentration, offers superior polishing efficiency, making it ideal for high-end applications. Middle Ce polishing powder balances performance and cost, suitable for general use. Low Ce polishing powder, with reduced cerium content, is more economical and often used for lower-grade polishing tasks, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
