<p><p>The augmented reality market is categorized into three main types: head-mounted displays (HMDs), head-up displays (HUDs), and handheld devices. HMDs offer immersive experiences by integrating digital content within a user's field of vision, suitable for gaming and training. HUDs project information onto a transparent display, often used in automotive and aviation settings for real-time data access. Handheld devices, such as smartphones and tablets, leverage camera capabilities to overlay digital information onto the physical world, popular in mobile applications and navigation.</p></p>
