<p><p>The Multi-Pixel Photon Counter (MPPC) market is categorized into two primary types based on detection capability: Single Photon Detection and Multiphoton Detection. Single Photon Detection focuses on capturing individual photons, making it ideal for low-light applications such as medical imaging and quantum communication. In contrast, Multiphoton Detection involves the simultaneous detection of multiple photons, enabling more efficient imaging and analysis in fields like particle physics and advanced sensing. Both types leverage the high sensitivity and fast response of MPPC technology for various applications.</p></p>
