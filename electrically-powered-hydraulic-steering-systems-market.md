<p><p>Electrically-Powered Hydraulic Steering Systems (EPHS) combine the benefits of traditional hydraulic steering with electric power assistance. These systems use an electric motor to drive a hydraulic pump, which provides the necessary pressure for steering assistance. This integration improves fuel efficiency, reduces emissions, and enhances steering precision compared to conventional hydraulic systems. EPHS is widely used in passenger vehicles, commercial vehicles, and off-highway equipment due to its reliability and performance.</p><p>The Electrically-Powered Hydraulic Steering Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period.
