<p><strong>The Wood and Furniture Paint Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood and furniture paint additives market is primarily categorized into solventborne and waterborne systems. Solventborne additives are based on organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, waterborne additives utilize water as a solvent, providing a more eco-friendly option with reduced volatile organic compounds (VOCs). These additives enhance properties like flow, leveling, and drying times, catering to diverse consumer preferences and regulatory requirements in the coating industry.</p></p>
