<p><p>The horizontal foldable screen mobile phone market can be categorized into two main types: fold outward laterally and fold inward laterally. Fold outward laterally devices expand by opening from the center, revealing a larger screen on the outside, enabling multi-tasking and enhanced media viewing. Both designs aim to balance portability with a larger display experience for users.</p></p>
