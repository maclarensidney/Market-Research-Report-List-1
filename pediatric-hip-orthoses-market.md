<p><p>The pediatric hip orthoses market is primarily categorized into fixed and hip articulated types. Fixed hip orthoses provide immobilization and stability for the hip joint, particularly useful in managing conditions like developmental dysplasia or post-surgical recovery. In contrast, hip articulated orthoses allow for free movement of the hip while maintaining necessary support, facilitating rehabilitation and functional development. These products cater to children with various orthopedic needs, promoting proper hip alignment and mobility during critical growth periods.</p></p>
