<p><p>The roof drains market encompasses various types, each serving specific drainage needs. Sill type drains are designed for flat roofs with a low-profile, while parapet type drains are fitted into vertical wall edges, ensuring efficient water flow. Flow control type drains manage the rate of water discharge to prevent overload, while overflow type drains provide additional safety by redirecting excess water during heavy rainfall. Other types may include specialized designs tailored for unique architectural features or specific environmental conditions.</p></p>
