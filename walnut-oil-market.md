<p><strong>The Walnut Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold-Pressed Walnut Oil</li><li>Expeller-Pressed Walnut Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The walnut oil market includes primarily two types: cold-pressed and expeller-pressed walnut oil. Cold-pressed walnut oil is extracted at low temperatures, preserving its flavor, aroma, and nutritional properties, making it ideal for salad dressings and finishing dishes. In contrast, expeller-pressed walnut oil is obtained through mechanical pressing without the use of solvents, resulting in slightly higher temperatures that may affect some delicate compounds. This type is often used for frying and cooking due to its higher smoke point.</p></p>
