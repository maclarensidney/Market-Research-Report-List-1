<p><ul><li>Drag Chain</li><li>Roller (driven or gravity)</li><li>Plastic Type</li><li>Slat Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom pallet conveyor market includes various types that cater to specific material handling needs. Drag chain conveyors utilize chains to move heavy pallets, ideal for harsh environments. Roller conveyors, either driven or gravity-operated, facilitate smooth movement and are versatile for different pallet sizes. Plastic type conveyors are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, suited for hygienic applications. Slat type conveyors offer flexibility in handling larger loads and are customizable for diverse applications, making them essential in manufacturing and distribution sectors.</p></p>
