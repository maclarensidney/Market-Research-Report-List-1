<p><p>The Probe Card PCB market features three primary types: PTH Type, Buildup IVH Type, and Hybrid Type. PTH (plated through-hole) Type utilizes traditional interconnections for reliable testing in various applications. Buildup IVH (interconnect via with buildup layer) Type offers advanced performance with multiple layers for intricate designs and improved signal integrity. Hybrid Type combines elements of both PTH and Buildup IVH to leverage the strengths of each, providing versatility and efficiency in semiconductor testing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
