<p><p>The mini desiccant bags market comprises three primary types: molecular sieve, activated carbon, and calcium chloride. Molecular sieves are highly porous materials that effectively absorb moisture and separate gases, making them ideal for preventing humidity damage. Activated carbon is used for its adsorption properties, capturing odors and volatile organic compounds while reducing moisture. Calcium chloride is a hygroscopic salt that absorbs significant amounts of water, offering efficient moisture control, especially in enclosed environments. Each type serves distinct applications based on moisture management needs.</p></p>
