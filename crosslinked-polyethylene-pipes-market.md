<p><strong>The Crosslinked Polyethylene Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEX-A Pipes</li><li>PEX-B Pipes</li><li>PEX-C Pipes</li><li>PEX-AL-PEX Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crosslinked polyethylene (PEX) pipes market comprises various types, each with distinct properties. PEX-A pipes offer higher flexibility and resistance through an extensive crosslinking process, ideal for cold-weather applications. PEX-B pipes are produced using a silane method, providing excellent chemical resistance and lower cost. PEX-C pipes, made through electron beam irradiation, are known for uniform crosslinking and durability. PEX-AL-PEX pipes combine PEX and aluminum, offering enhanced strength and thermal performance, suitable for both hot and cold water applications.</p></p>
