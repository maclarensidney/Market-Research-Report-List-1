<p><p>The high-end hand dryer market comprises two main types: hot air dryers and jet air dryers. Hot air dryers utilize heated air to evaporate moisture from hands, offering a slow but gentle drying experience. In contrast, jet air dryers use powerful, focused streams of air to rapidly remove water, providing a quicker drying process with less energy consumption. Both types cater to environmentally conscious consumers and are often found in upscale restrooms, emphasizing hygiene and efficiency.</p></p>
