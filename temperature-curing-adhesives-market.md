<p><p>The temperature curing adhesives market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Water-based Adhesives**: These utilize water as a solvent, offering low odor and environmental benefits.</p><p>2. **Solvent-based Adhesives**: Comprising organic solvents, they provide strong bonding but pose VOC concerns.</p><p>3. **Hot Melt Adhesives**: Applied in a molten state, they cool to form strong bonds quickly, ideal for high-speed applications.</p><p>4. **Pressure Sensitive Adhesives**: These adhere upon contact, reverse easily, and are widely used in labels and tapes, offering versatility and convenience.</p></p>
