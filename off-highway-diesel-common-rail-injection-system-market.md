<p><ul><li>Conventional Solenoid Injector</li><li>Piezoelectric Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Off-highway Diesel Common Rail Injection System market features two primary types of injectors: Conventional Solenoid Injectors and Piezoelectric Injectors. Conventional Solenoid Injectors utilize electromagnetic solenoids to control fuel injection timing and quantity, offering reliability and cost-effectiveness for various applications. In contrast, Piezoelectric Injectors use piezoelectric crystals to enable faster and more precise fuel injection, resulting in improved engine performance and efficiency. Both types are crucial for optimizing power output and reducing emissions in off-highway diesel engines.</p></p>
