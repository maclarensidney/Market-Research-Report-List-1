<p><p>The Super Clear Glass market is primarily divided into two types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in varied thickness and surface textures, making it ideal for architectural applications. In contrast, Float Glass is created by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a smooth, uniform surface and high optical clarity. Both types cater to diverse needs in construction, automotive, and consumer products, emphasizing quality and aesthetics.</p></p>
