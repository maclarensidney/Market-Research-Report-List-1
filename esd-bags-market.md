<p><p>ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) bags are used to protect electronic components from static damage. The market includes Conductive and Dissipative Polymers, Metal, and Others. Conductive polymers allow static to flow across their surface, safely grounding charges. Dissipative polymers slow static discharge, preventing sudden surges. Metal bags, often aluminum, provide a Faraday cage effect, shielding contents from external static. Other materials include composite or hybrid solutions tailored for specific needs. Each type ensures safe handling, storage, and transport of sensitive electronics by controlling static electricity.</p></p>
