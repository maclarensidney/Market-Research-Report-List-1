<p><p>The OLED encapsulation materials market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Rigid glass offers high durability and excellent barrier properties, making it ideal for traditional displays. Flexible glass provides a lightweight alternative for bendable devices. Conformal coatings, applied directly onto components, protect against moisture and contaminants. Metal foils offer robust protection and are suitable for flexible displays. Laminates combine multiple layers for enhanced protection and flexibility, catering to diverse design requirements in the evolving OLED industry.</p></p>
