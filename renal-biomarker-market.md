<p><p>The renal biomarker market encompasses various types that aid in diagnosing and monitoring kidney diseases. Functional biomarkers assess the operational status of renal functions, indicating efficiency in filtration and excretion. Up-regulated proteins highlight specific proteins that increase in response to kidney damage or disease, serving as indicators of pathological changes. Other biomarkers may include genetic markers, metabolites, and imaging-based indicators that provide additional insights into kidney health, aiding in personalized treatment strategies and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
