<p><strong>The Enzyme Inhibitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversible Inhibitor</li><li>Irreversible Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enzyme inhibitor market is primarily divided into two types: reversible and irreversible inhibitors. Reversible inhibitors bind temporarily to enzymes, allowing their activity to return once the inhibitor is removed; they often serve as competitive, non-competitive, or uncompetitive inhibitors. In contrast, irreversible inhibitors form permanent bonds with enzymes, permanently deactivating them and typically leading to long-lasting effects. Both categories play crucial roles in drug development and therapeutic applications, targeting specific biochemical pathways for various diseases.</p></p>
